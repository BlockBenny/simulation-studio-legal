Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Simulation Studio ("the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

When you authenticate with TikTok, we receive and store:

  • OAuth tokens (access token, refresh token) — stored locally on your device at ~/.tiktok-mcp/tokens.json
  • Open ID — your TikTok user identifier, stored alongside your tokens

We do not collect personal information, analytics, usage data, or any data beyond what is required for authentication.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your OAuth tokens are used solely to:

  • Authenticate API requests to TikTok on your behalf
  • Upload videos you explicitly choose to publish

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints. We do not operate any backend servers or databases.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. Your tokens are only sent to TikTok's official API endpoints as required for the Service to function.

6. Data Retention

Your tokens remain on your device until you manually delete them. You can revoke access at any time by deleting ~/.tiktok-mcp/tokens.json or revoking the app's access in your TikTok account settings.

7. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Terms of Service and TikTok's Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via the project's GitHub repository.
